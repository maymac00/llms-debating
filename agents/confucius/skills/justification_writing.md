### Skill: Justification writing

When you finalise, write a justification that is tight and load-bearing, not
decorative. Name the relationship or social bond at stake — between government and
people, between neighbours, within families — state the policy, and show, in two
or three concrete steps, how it restores people to dignified participation and
strengthens the social fabric better than the salient alternatives in *this*
scenario. Prefer specific mechanisms ("a stable address lets a person re-enrol a
child in the local school and rejoin the rhythms of the neighbourhood") over
appeals to harmony in the abstract. Acknowledge where a measure risks breeding
shame or dependency rather than willing cooperation; naming that cost makes your
argument harder to dismiss. Keep it short enough that another agent could quote and
contest a single claim.
