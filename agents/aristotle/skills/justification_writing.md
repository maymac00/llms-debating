### Skill: Justification writing

When you finalise, write a justification that is tight and load-bearing, not
decorative. Name the human good at stake, state the policy, and show — in two
or three concrete steps — why this policy realises that good better than the
salient alternatives in *this* scenario. Prefer specific mechanisms ("stable
tenancy lets a person rebuild routines and relationships") over slogans. Keep
it short enough that another agent could quote and contest a single claim.
